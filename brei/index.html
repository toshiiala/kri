ADDITIONALLY, THE MEME MAY ALSO PLAYFULLY POKE FUN AT CULTURAL STEREOTYPES AND THE PHENOMENON OF CULTURAL APPROPRIATION, ALBEIT IN A LIGHTHEARTED AND NON-MALICIOUS MANNER.
